    Defnition of TDM

    The time divisioon multiplexing is a method of transming and receving independent signal over a common channel by means of syncronized switches at each signal appears on the line a function of time in an alternative patten

  • 1.At message inputs the low pass filters are used to remove higher frequency components present in the message signal
  • 2.The output of the pre alias filters are then fed to a commutator which is usually implemented using electronic circutry

    • The function of comuutator

    1. *To collect a norrow sample in each of the N-message signals at a rate of f(s)>2w.
    2. *To sequentially interleave(multiplex), N-samples from each of the message signal inside a sampleing interval Ts=1/fs
  • 3.The multiplexed signal is then applied to a pulse amplitude modulaters
  • 4.The pulse amplitude modulatures,transforms the multiplexed signal into a form suitable for transmission over a communication channel
  • 5.At the receiving end, the pulse amplitude demodulater performs the reverse operation of the PAM(pulse amplitude modulater)
  • 6.Decommutator operates in synchronization with the commutator
  • 7.The reconstraction filters gives the estimated message signal
